What is Word Counter?

Word Counter is a writing utility that analyzes your text in real time. It shows total words, characters, sentences, paragraphs, estimated reading time, and keyword density so you can optimize your content quickly.

How it helps content creators?

Content creators often need to hit specific word limits, improve readability, and avoid keyword stuffing. This tool helps you write with better structure, stronger SEO balance, and faster editing decisions.

  • Track writing goals for blogs, scripts, captions, and assignments.
  • Estimate reader attention using reading-time insights.
  • Improve on-page SEO by monitoring frequently used keywords.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is this word counter free to use?

Yes. The tool is completely free and works directly in your browser.

2. Does my text get uploaded anywhere?

No. All counting and analysis happen on your device using client-side JavaScript only.

3. How is reading time calculated?

Reading time is estimated using an average speed of 200 words per minute.

4. Why are common words missing in keyword density?

Common stop words like "the" and "and" are excluded so you can focus on meaningful keywords in your text.

5. Can I use this tool on mobile?

Yes. The interface is fully responsive and optimized for phones and tablets.
